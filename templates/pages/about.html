Our Mission

Nepal's job market is fragmented across dozens of government websites, private portals, and social media pages. Job seekers waste hours checking multiple sources, missing deadlines, and falling for misleading ads.

NepHub solves this. We aggregate verified opportunities from official government ministries, the Public Service Commission, DOFE-approved agencies, and trusted private employers — all in one place, updated daily.

We believe every Nepali deserves access to good employment information without paying a rupee. NepHub is and will always be completely free for job seekers.
